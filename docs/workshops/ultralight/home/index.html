Schedule

Week 1.
January 14, 1-5pm
with Laurel Schwulst
"Lightweight Website" ... HTML, CSS, publishing

before:
bring a strong & simple text / content you'd like to publish

- welcome
- introductions
- introduce workshop page (this site)
- exercise 1: workshop homepage
- microlecture 1: ultralight
- microlecture 2: html
- microlecture 3: css
- exercise 2: website that can also be a reading, but keep it light

after:
Finish your one page, text-only website, or make it better. At the beginning of workshop next week, read it aloud and / or share it.

Week 2.
January 21, 12-4pm
with Marie Otsuka
"Websites with Downtime" ... JavaScript with times & dates

before:
bring a concept for a website that's only available at certain times

Week 3.
January 28, 1-5pm
with Benjamin Earl
"Ultralight Computer" ... physical computing, "coding in situ"

before:
bring any ideas around what's useful information to experience in a specific location
