About

üçä fruitful.school was an independently-run workshop series founded in 2020 by laurel schwulst and john provencher. It explored how contemporary artistic practices engaged with the world wide web or technology at large.

üçä fruitful.school ended in 2024. In 2025, Laurel continues to teach through her new umbrella educational project called Ultralight School, which took root in her 2024 fruitful workshop of the same name.
